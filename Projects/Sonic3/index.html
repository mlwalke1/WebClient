Sonic the Hedgehog 3
by mlwalke1

A blue hedgehog who collects golden rings

One of my favorite games growing up was Sonic the Hedgehog 3 for Sega Genesis.

Characters

Sonic the Hedgehog

Sonic is the main protagonist of the game. He's a blue hedgehog who collects golden rings and runs super fast.

Dr. Ivo "Eggman" Robotnik

Dr. Robotnik, or Dr. Eggman, is the main antagonist of the Sonic the Hedgehog series. He uses machines and robots, try try to take over the world, but is thwarted by Sonic.

Miles "Tails" Prower

Tails, has two tails which allows him to fly like a helicopter and assist Sonic. You can play as Tails in two player mode, or have him follow you around in one player mode.

Knuckles the Echidna

Knuckles is named after his big knuckles. He is initially an antagonist because he is tricked by Dr. Robotnik, but you can play as him in the second half of the game.

Zones

Angel Island Zone

The opening zone of the game. Angel island is a tropical forest island with waterfalls. The second act of the level the forest is covered in flames.

  • More

Hydrocity Zone

Hydrocity Zone is a mostly underwater zone. Sonic can drown when he's underwater. The music begins to intensify and you must find water bubbles to get sonic a breath of air.

  • More

Marble Garden Zone

Back above ground Sonic enters the Marble Garden Zone. It features broken pillars and ruins. There's also a spinning disc that Sonic can fly on and controls it with his running speed.

  • More

Carnival Night Zone

This zone is, suprise, carnival themed, and at night. You spin on colorful cylinders and listen to carnival music, while navigating through the level.

  • More

Ice Cap Zone

The ice cap zone was one of my favorite because you start off snowboarding. After that you proceed into ice caverns where many of the structures in the level are frozen in ice blocks.

  • More

Launch Base Zone

This is the final zone of Sonic the Hedgehog 3, without the Knuckles game extension. Sonic and company chase Dr. Robotnik into his base the death egg. The end of the level Sonic faces Dr. Robotnik in the sky as the base launches off.
